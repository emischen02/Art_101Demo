The Arena of Legends

Chapter II: Weapons of Destiny

Falchion

Type: Divine Blade

Length: 42 inches

Weight: 2.8 pounds

Special Properties: Glows with divine light, never dulls

History: Legendary sword wielded by Marth, Hero-King of Altea.

VS

Sword of the Creator

Type: Relic Weapon

Length: Variable (can extend)

Weight: 12.5 pounds

Special Properties: Can extend/retract, unbreakable, channels divine power

History: Ancient weapon wielded by Byleth, the Ashen Demon.

The weapons master approached, carrying two ornate chests that seemed to pulse with ancient power.

Marth's eyes gleamed as the first chest opened, revealing the Falchion. The sword seemed to sing as it caught the light, its blade shimmering with an otherworldly blue glow. The hilt was wrapped in silver wire, and the pommel bore the crest of the royal house of Altea.

Marth:

"Ah, Falchion. I've heard the legends. They say it was forged by the divine dragon Naga herself."

As he grasped the weapon, the blade erupted in brilliant blue light, and the very air around him seemed to shimmer with magical energy. The crowd gasped as runes along the blade's edge began to glow with ancient script.

Meanwhile, Byleth's chest opened with a thunderous boom, revealing the Sword of the Creator. The massive blade was forged from a metal that seemed to absorb light rather than reflect it, and the handle was carved from the heartwood of an ancient tree blessed by the goddess.

Byleth:

"Sword of the Creator... a relic of legend, said to be a gift from the goddess herself. This blade has never known defeat."

As his hands closed around the weapon, divine energy began to gather overhead, and the first drops of rain began to fall. Lightning crackled along the blade's edge, and the ground beneath Byleth's feet began to crack and splinter.

The weapons master stepped back, his face pale with awe. "Never in my fifty years have I seen such power. These are not mere weapons—they are instruments of destiny."

The Final Preparations

Both warriors make their final preparations, their minds focused on the coming battle.

Marth's Strategy

  • Uses speed and agility to outmaneuver his opponent
  • Plans to strike at weak points in armor
  • Relies on Falchion's divine properties for enhanced precision
  • Will attempt to tire out the larger warrior

Byleth's Strategy

  • Uses overwhelming force to break through defenses
  • Plans to use the Sword of the Creator's weight and reach to advantage
  • Will attempt to end the fight quickly with devastating blows
  • Relies on superior strength and tactical skill
